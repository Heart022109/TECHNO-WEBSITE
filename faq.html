Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are your products keto or diabetic-friendly?

A: Many of our offerings are low in sugar and made with natural sweeteners like coconut sugar and honey. We also accommodate custom orders for specific dietary needs.

Q: Do you offer delivery or meet-ups?

A: Yes! We offer scheduled deliveries within Metro Manila and nearby areas. Pick-up and meet-up options are also available depending on location.

Q: Are your ingredients organic?

A: We prioritize clean-label, high-quality ingredients, some of which are organic and locally sourced. We do not use artificial preservatives or flavorings.

Q: How should I store your pastries to keep them fresh?

A: Most of our products are best enjoyed fresh but can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for 2–3 days or refrigerated for up to a week.

Q: Do you cater to events or bulk orders?

A: Yes, we accept bulk orders for parties, corporate events, and special occasions. Custom packaging and flavors are available upon request.

Q: Do your products contain allergens like nuts or dairy?

A: Some of our items contain common allergens such as nuts, eggs, and dairy. Ingredients are listed in each product description. Let us know if you have allergies — we’re happy to recommend options.
